Life Simulator

Every Life has a story. What will yours be?

How to Play

  1. Click the "Take Birth!" button to start your life journey.
  2. Make choices at each stage of life and see how they affect your story.
  3. Experience random events that can change your path.
  4. You need to finish each scenario to move onto the next and age.
  5. Try to live a long and fulfilling life!
  6. At the end of your life, view your personalized life report and share it with friends!
